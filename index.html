Cannabis and Menstrual Cramps

Cannabis is taken by some women to alleviate or lessen the pain from menstrual cramps. It thus acts as an analgesic, combatting bouts of searing pain experienced during cramping. Across the globe, women use cannabis to treat menstrual cramps.
